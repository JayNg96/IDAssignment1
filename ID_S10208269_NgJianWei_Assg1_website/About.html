About Me

Hello! I'm JianWei. I've always been a story-teller first, long before i stumbled upon photography. It all started with writing silly imaginary pieces when I was younger, until the day i picked up a camera and discovered another medium to express myself. Capturing emotions is one of the most imporant parts of my work. I feel strongly about making the photographs that i can connect with. Even though it is the emotions of my subjects that I'm documenting. I find a reflection of myself in them. There's no surprise that photograph is the love of my life, but traveling is also the blood in my vein. I'm endlessly inspired by nature and adventures. I love waking up to new horizons, makin friends with people, an exploring the world.
